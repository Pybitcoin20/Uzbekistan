This document outlines the viral growth engine designed to acquire the first 1,000 users and convert them into paying customers.

## 1. TikTok Content Engine (Viral Machine)
**Goal:** 2 videos per day to drive traffic to the platform.

### Content Pillars:
1.  **"Hidden Gems"**: Show places nobody knows about (e.g., "The secret underground mosque in Bukhara").
2.  **"Plov Culture"**: High-energy videos of massive Plov cauldrons with "Plov Scores".
3.  **"AI Magic"**: Screen recordings of the AI Trip Planner creating a 7-day luxury itinerary in 5 seconds.

### Viral Hooks:
- "Don't visit Uzbekistan until you see this..."
- "I found the most beautiful place on the Silk Road."
- "Stop planning your trips manually. Use this AI instead."
- "The best Plov in Tashkent is NOT where you think it is."

### CTA Strategy:
- "Link in bio to plan your trip for free 🇺🇿"
- "Check your Plov Score on our website!"

## 5. Analytics Dashboard (Admin)
**Track these metrics:**
- **K-Factor**: Number of new users generated by each existing user (Referral success).
- **Conversion Rate**: Landing page visitor -> Registered user.
- **Churn Rate**: Premium subscription cancellations.
- **Points Velocity**: How fast users are earning/spending points.
